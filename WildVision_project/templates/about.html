About Wild Vision

AI-Powered Wildlife Detection for a Safer and Smarter Future

Our Mission

Wild Vision is designed to revolutionize wildlife protection by using advanced AI vision systems to detect and alert human settlements of wild animal intrusions. Our goal is to minimize human–animal conflicts, prevent crop damage, and safeguard lives through real-time detection and instant alerting mechanisms.

Core Objectives

  • Develop a robust AI model capable of recognizing multiple wild species in real environments.
  • Enable real-time monitoring through video surveillance and live detection.
  • Provide instant SMS alerts and siren notifications for early warnings.
  • Integrate seamlessly with low-cost edge devices like Raspberry Pi or Jetson Nano.

Technology Stack

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS (Bootstrap 5)
  • Backend: Flask (Python)
  • AI Model: YOLOv11 (Ultralytics)
  • Alert System: Twilio API & Siren Sound Module
  • Deployment: Edge Devices / Cloud

Future Enhancements

Future developments include integrating drone-based live tracking, GPS-based animal movement mapping, mobile app alerts, and cloud-enabled data analytics for wildlife behavior prediction. Wild Vision envisions a smarter and safer coexistence between humans and nature.
